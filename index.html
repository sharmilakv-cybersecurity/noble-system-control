Gas Suppression System

Gas suppression systems are waterless, automatic fire protection systems designed for sensitive environments like server rooms and laboratories. They extinguish fires within seconds by releasing inert gases or clean agent chemicals that lower oxygen levels or absorb heat without leaving residue, protecting equipment and allowing for rapid, safe deployment in high-value areas.

Learn More

Water Leakage System

Water leakage systems are safety mechanisms designed to detect, monitor, and often automatically shut off water flow to prevent water damage in residential or commercial buildings. They utilize sensors, smart meters, or acoustic technology to identify leaks ranging from minor drips to major pipe bursts.

Learn More

Rodent System

Ultrasonic rodent repellent systems emit high-frequency sound waves that create an uncomfortable environment for rodents, effectively keeping them away from critical IT infrastructure, server rooms, and electrical panels without the use of harmful chemicals.

Learn More

Fire Hydrant & Sprinkler System

A fire hydrant system is an active, high-pressure water network comprising pipes, pumps, and valves designed for rapid fire suppression in industrial, commercial, and residential buildings. It acts as a primary firefighting tool by providing instant access to water from municipal supplies or private reservoirs for firefighters to use via hoses. A sprinkler system is a network of pipes containing pressurized water, equipped with automatic sprinklers that release water when a fire occurs.

Learn More

Fire Extinguishers

Portable and specialized fire extinguishers designed for instant response to various classes of fires. We supply and install powder, CO2, foam, and water-based extinguishers to ensure comprehensive safety compliance and protection.

Learn More

Auto Glow & LED Signages
